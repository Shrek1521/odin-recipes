Roasted Tomato and Red Pepper Soup

(Meal Prep ~27 Blocks)


Ingredients

  • 1.5kg cherry tomatoes or use big tomatoes with the top stem connection cut out. Cut them all in half
  • 2 red onions, cut into quarters
  • 3 red peppers, seeds and top removed
  • 1 whole garlic head, peeled
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh thyme
  • Generous amount of olive oil.
  • 2 white onions
  • 30g butter
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato puree
  • 660ml chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cayenne pepper powder to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (smoked or sweet)
  • Half tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Half teaspoon of sugar
  • Handful of basil
  • 120ml double cream
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. In a large tray mix the tomatoes, garlic, peppers, red onion, rosemary and thyme, add generous amount of olive oil, salt and pepper and roast in a preheated oven, fan on 190C until caramelized, about 50-60 minutes.
  2. When ready, saute the white onion in the butter in a fairly large pot, add the tomato paste, mix it and add all the roasted tomatoes from the tray.
  3. Add the stock, cayenne pepper, smoked paprika, balsamic vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper, if needed and the fresh basil. Let it simmer on the stove for 8-10 minutes. Then add the cream and cook for 5 more mins.
  4. Taste it and season with more salt and pepper, and add sugar if needed.

A Little Bit Extra

Serve with toast with cheese of your choice, I've used mozzarella and cheddar! (Take some bread, butter both sides, put cheese a slice of cheddar and grated motzarella in between, and fry it on a pan with butter on both sides)
