(approx 26/27 blocks)


Method & Ingredients


Prep and then food-process all of the following together:

  • 600g basil (washed)
  • 300g pine nuts (toast them before processing)
  • 18 Garlic cloves (approx 1.5 bulbs)
  • Juice of 4 lemons
  • 300g parmesan
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • Pepper 160 turns
  • 400ml extra virgin olive oil
