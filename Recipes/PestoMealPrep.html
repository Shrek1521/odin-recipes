(approx 26/27 blocks)


Method & Ingredients


Prep and then food-process all of the following together:

  • 600g basil (£9.60/£1.60 each bunch)
  • 300g pine nuts (lightly toasted) (£11.70/£3.90)
  • 18 Garlic cloves (1.5 bulbs) (£1.30/£0.65 each)
  • Juice of 4 lemons (£1.20/£0.30 each)
  • 300g parmesan (£5.50)
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • Pepper 160 turns
  • 400ml extra virgin olive oil (£3-£4)
